Gourmet Bliss Menu

Welcome to Gourmet Bliss! Discover our delicious selection of appetizers, main courses, and desserts, carefully crafted to delight your senses.


Appetizers

Start your meal with our tasty appetizers, perfect for awakening your taste buds.

  • Cheese Platter

    A luxurious selection of fine cheeses, including creamy brie, aged cheddar, and tangy blue cheese,
    served with honey, walnuts, and crispy artisan crackers.

  • Bruschetta

    runchy toasted baguette slices topped with a vibrant mix of fresh tomatoes, fragrant basil, garlic,
    and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil. A simple yet flavorful classic.

  • Stuffed Mushrooms

    Juicy mushroom caps filled with a creamy blend of ricotta cheese, garlic, and herbs,
    then baked until golden brown and irresistibly tender.


Main Courses

Enjoy our carefully prepared main dishes, made with the finest ingredients and authentic flavors.

  • Grilled Ribeye Steak

    A succulent hand-cut ribeye steak, seasoned to perfection and grilled over
    an open flame, finished with a rich garlic butter sauce and served with rosemary potatoes.

  • Pizza

    A delicious oven-baked dish with a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, and melted cheese, topped with a variety of ingredients.
    Our Selections:
    Margherita- Fresh mozzarella, basil, and tomato sauce.
    Pepperoni- Spicy pepperoni slices over gooey cheese.
    Four Cheese- A blend of mozzarella, parmesan, gorgonzola, and ricotta.
    Vegetarian- A mix of fresh vegetables like bell peppers, mushrooms, and olives.
    BBQ Chicken- Grilled chicken with smoky barbecue sauce.

  • Vegetarian Lasagna

    A hearty, layered dish of oven-roasted vegetables, creamy ricotta cheese,
    and homemade tomato sauce, baked to golden perfection and served with a side of garlic bread.


Desserts

End your meal with a delightful dessert, the perfect sweet finish to your dining experience.

  • Chocolate Lava Cake

    A rich, decadent chocolate cake with a warm molten center,
    served with a scoop of velvety vanilla and a drizzle of dark chocolate sauce.

  • Strawberry Cheesecake

    A creamy, New York-style cheesecake on a buttery graham cracker crust,

  • Tiramisu

    beloved Italian classic, featuring layers of espresso-soaked ladyfingers, smooth mascarpone cream,
